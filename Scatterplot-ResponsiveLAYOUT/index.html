Data Visualization of Planets in the Solar System

This is a little exploration of a data set that can be found on Kaggle. The data is filtered to observations that are for planets in the solar system. Some values had to be adjusted as they were innacurate.

The scatterplot maps the planets in relation to their density and and gravity.
